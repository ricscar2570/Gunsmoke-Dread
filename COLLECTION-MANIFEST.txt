HORROR OVER ACTION:
The collection prioritizes psychological horror and atmosphere over 
combat-heavy encounters. Combat exists to raise stakes, not be primary focus.

CHOICES MATTER:
Every major decision has consequences - mechanical, narrative, or both. 
No "correct path", only different costs.

COSMIC INSIGNIFICANCE:
Central theme is Lovecraftian realization of humanity's temporary place 
in universe. PG grow powerful but realize how small they are.

GM FLEXIBILITY:
Designed to be adapted. GMs encouraged to change, add, remove based on 
their group's preferences. Guidelines, not mandates.

RESPECTFUL HORROR:
Horror through atmosphere and implication, not graphic violence or 
exploitation. Mature themes handled with care.
